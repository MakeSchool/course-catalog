### Retention of Student Records

Make School maintains electronic records of the name, address, email address, and telephone number
of each student who is enrolled in an educational program. Make School permanently maintains
transcript for each student granted a degree

In addition, Make School maintains data, for a period of not less than five years, all of the
following information:

- The educational programs offered by the institution and the curriculum for each,

- The names and addresses of the members of the institution’s faculty and records of the educational
  qualifications of each member of the faculty, and

- Any other records required to be maintained by this chapter, including, but not limited to,
  records maintained pursuant to Article 16 which includes completion, placement and licensure rates
  as reported on Make School’s School Performance Fact Sheet, as applicable and annual reports as
  submitted to the Bureau.
